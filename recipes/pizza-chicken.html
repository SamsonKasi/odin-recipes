Pizza Chicken

Description

Pizza Chicken is a delicious fusion dish that combines the juicy tenderness of baked chicken breasts with the classic flavors of pizza. Topped with marinara sauce, melted mozzarella, and your favorite pizza toppings, this meal is both comforting and easy to prepare.

Perfect for weeknight dinners or when you’re craving pizza but want something a bit more protein-packed. Serve it with a side salad or garlic bread for a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 large boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced pepperoni or your favorite pizza toppings
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Fresh basil for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Lightly grease a baking dish with olive oil.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and oregano on both sides.
  3. Place the chicken in the prepared baking dish and bake for 20 minutes.
  4. Remove the chicken from the oven. Spoon marinara sauce over each breast, then top with mozzarella cheese and your chosen pizza toppings.
  5. Return to the oven and bake for another 10-15 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Garnish with fresh basil if desired. Serve hot and enjoy!
