Veggie Stir Fry

Description

Veggie Stir Fry is a vibrant and healthy dish packed with colorful vegetables and tossed in a savory sauce. It’s quick to prepare and perfect for a light lunch or dinner.

Serve it over steamed rice or noodles for a satisfying, plant-based meal that’s full of flavor and nutrients.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch mixed with 2 tablespoons water
  • Sesame seeds for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, sauté for 1 minute.
  3. Add broccoli, bell pepper, snap peas, and carrot. Stir fry for 4-5 minutes until just tender.
  4. Add soy sauce and cornstarch mixture. Stir until sauce thickens and coats the vegetables.
  5. Garnish with sesame seeds and serve over rice or noodles.
